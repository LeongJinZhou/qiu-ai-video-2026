// Introduction

THE FUTURE STARTS HERE

Artificial Intelligence is reshaping everything — from education to media, marketing to production. The QIU AI Video Innovation Challenge gives every student, regardless of faculty, hands-on experience with cutting-edge AI video generation tools.

Create a maximum 3-minute promotional or experiential video about QIU — its campus, student life, academics, or culture — using AI-powered platforms. Compete solo or in a team of up to 5. Total RM5,000 worth of Prizes.

🚀 Virtual Sprint
Fully online. Attend the kick-off briefing on Google Meet, create your video from anywhere, and submit via Google Drive before the deadline.
💡 Free AI Credits
All participants receive a complimentary Google AI Pro trial — you'll learn to claim it during the opening briefing. No prior AI experience needed.
👥 All Faculties Welcome
Open to all faculties — Computing, Business, Medicine, Pharmacy, Social Sciences, and Integrated Life Sciences. Your creativity is the only prerequisite.
🌱 SDG Aligned
Supporting UN Sustainable Development Goals: SDG 4 (Quality Education), SDG 8 (Decent Work & Growth), and SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure).

// Roadmap

THE ROAD TO GLORY

22 JUNE 2026 · 8:30 AM

Kick-off Briefing

1.5-hour briefing session via Google Meet. Tutorial on AI video tools, prompt engineering basics, and activation of Google AI Pro trial credits.

22 JUNE – 5 JULY 2026

Creation Sprint

Two full weeks to design your AI video masterpiece. Use any AI rendering software. Video duration: 1 to 3 minutes.

5 JULY 2026 · 11:59 PM

Submission Deadline

Upload your final video rendering to your assigned student Google Drive folder. Late uploads will not be counted.

6 – 13 JULY 2026

Judging Period

Official panel evaluation from QIU Student Life, Digital Communication Department, and external industry advisor.

14 JULY 2026

Winner Announcement

Winners announced on QIU official channels. Total prize pool RM5,000.

// Regulations

COMPETITION RULES

01

Open exclusively to registered QIU students (Solo or teams of 2 to 5 members).

02

Videos must align with the pre-approved QIU themes (Theme A to D).

03

Submission length must be between 1 minute minimum and 3 minutes maximum.

04

At least 80% of final video footage and rendering must be generated using AI systems.
